Lentils in Butter Sauce

Ingredients

  • Red Lentils
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Clarified Butter
  • Slit Green Chilli
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Chopped fresh chillies
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Mustard seeds
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Wash the lentils in running water and drain
  2. In a heavy bottomed pot, head the ghee and add in cumin seeds
  3. When the seeds crackle, add in the lentils and stir them in the pot until they are opaque
  4. Add the turmeric powder, the salt and the slit green chilli
  5. Stir for two more minutes and then add about 300ml of water
  6. Reduce the heat and simmer the lentils until they are soft and mashed
  7. Taste and adjust the seasoning, if required
  8. In a pan, heat the oil and add in the mustard seeds and chopped fresh chillies
  9. When seeds crackle, pour oil spice mixture over the lentils
  10. Finish with the chopped coriander leaves
  11. Serve hot
