Paneer simmered in creamy spinach gravy

Ingredients

  • Spinach Leaves, rinsed clean
  • Shredded ginger
  • Chopped fresh chilli
  • Turmeric powder
  • Paneer cut into cubes
  • Green Peas
  • Red Chilli powder
  • Oil
  • Cream
  • Salt
  • Ghee/clarified butter

Steps

  1. In a pot, boil the spinach with some salt, the ginger, the fresh chilli, and the turmeric powder
  2. When soft, drain off the excess water and cool the spinach under cold, running water to retain the color
  3. Puree the spinach in a food processor
  4. In a heavy bottomed pot, heat some oil and add the cumin seeds
  5. When seeds crackle, add the ground spinach and stir over high heat
  6. Continue stirring until spinach begins to thicken and appears almost dry
  7. Add in cream, the chilli powder, paneer cubes, green peas, and a bit of salt
  8. Reduce the heat to simmer
  9. After a few minutes, taste and adjust the seasoning
  10. When the gravy begins to buble, and is thick and creamy, remove from heat
  11. Serve hot
