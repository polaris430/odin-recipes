Honey and Lemon Cooler

Ingredients

  • Honey
  • Juice of lemons
  • Mint Leaves

Steps

  1. Simmer 700ml cups of water with honey
  2. Stir until honey is melted
  3. Remove from heat & set aside to cool
  4. Add lemon juice & sprinkle mint leaves
  5. Serve over ice or refrigerate until chilled
